About

FarmPlace is an agriculture opportunity hub for unemployed youths with keen interest in agribusiness. FarmPlace recruits and trains graduates in different agricultural ventures and proper farm business management practices to equip them with skills to start and operate a profitable agribusiness. FarmPlace partners with companies to provide grants and support to Fellows of FarmPlace to explore untapped agricultural opportunities. FarmPlace aims to tackle problem of food security and reducing unemployment by grooming youths interested in agribusiness.

Connect

This will be a hub to connect young people to agricultural internships and trainings so as to gain hands-on experience.

Educate

Farmplace.com.ng will also serve as a hub to educate and enlighten young people about the positives of agriculture as a business.

Finance

Crowdsourced funds will be used to give grants to intending farmers that must have undergone trainings using the platform.
