Find Agricultural Internships and Training Around You

This is a platform to train young people in different agricultural ventures and proper farm business management practices to equip them with skills to start and operate a profitable agribusiness.

What we do

Farmplace is a social endeavour that seeks to increase interest of youths in the agricultural sector as a means to feed the nation and as well reduce unemployment in the society.

Connect

This will be a hub to connect young people to agricultural internships and trainings so as to gain hands-on experience

Educate

Farmplace will also serve as a hub to educate and enlighten young people about the positives of agriculture as a business

Finance

FarmPlace partners with companies to provide grants to young people to explore untapped agricultural opportunities
